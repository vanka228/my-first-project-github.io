Burger

A burger is a dish made of a cooked ground meat patty, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun with ingredients like cheese, lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, and sauces. It is popular for its simplicity and ability to be easily customized, and can also be made with chicken, fish, or plant-based alternatives. The modern burger originated in the United States in the early 20th century, influenced by German immigrants who brought “Hamburg-style” minced beef. It later became a global fast-food icon, especially after the rise of fast-food chains, and is now enjoyed worldwide in many different variations.
